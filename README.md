## What is HireLoom?

HireLoom is a macOS desktop app that sits alongside your interview and helps you perform your best — without anyone knowing it's there.

### Live Interview Mode
HireLoom listens to your interview audio in real-time, transcribes the interviewer's questions, and generates smart, resume-aware answers in a floating overlay.

- Works with **Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, WebEx** — any app that plays audio
- **Stealth Mode** — completely invisible to screen shares, recordings & screenshots
- **Resume-aware** — AI references your real experience, projects & skills
- **PIP overlay** — drag anywhere on screen, always on top

### Screen Assist Mode
AI reads your screen and solves questions for you — perfect for online assessments and coding tests.

- **Auto-detects MCQs** from screenshots every 3 seconds
- Works with **HireVue, TestGorilla**, and any screen-based assessment
- Shows the **best answer + explanation** with typewriter animation
- Pick a specific app window or entire screen

### AI Mock Interviews
Practice with a voice-driven AI interviewer that asks questions, listens to your answers, and scores your performance.

- **Behavioral, Technical, System Design & Case Study** question types
- **Easy, Medium, Hard** difficulty levels
- Real-time scoring after each answer
- Detailed performance reports saved automatically

### Resume Review
Upload your resume and get an AI-powered analysis with ATS compatibility score, skills match, and actionable feedback.

### Interview Calendar
Schedule and track your interviews with Google Calendar sync, color-coded events, and quick stats.

## Privacy & Security

- **All audio is processed in real-time and discarded** — nothing is stored on our servers
- **Your resume stays on your device** — parsed locally, only sent during active sessions
- **Stealth Mode** uses Apple's ScreenCaptureKit exclusion API — the app window is excluded from all capture at the system level

## FAQ

**Can interviewers detect HireLoom?**
No. Stealth Mode makes the app completely invisible to screen recordings, screenshots, and screen sharing at the macOS system level.

**Which meeting platforms does it work with?**
All of them — Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, WebEx, and any app that plays audio through your Mac.

**Does it work on Windows?**
Currently macOS only. Windows support is on our roadmap.

**Is it free?**
Yes! HireLoom is completely free during early access.
